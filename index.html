About

Hey, I’m Afrin — a self-taught video editor with over 3 years of experience crafting visuals that tell stories close to my heart.

What started as a hobby quickly turned into a passion. I make videos for myself — edits that reflect what I feel, what I love, and what inspires me. From AMVs to aesthetic beach vibes, and raw cinematic cuts of cars and bikes, each project is a piece of my world.

Even though I’ve mostly edited for myself, I’m always open to connecting with like-minded creators. If you have a vision, a vibe, or a story you want to bring to life, let’s talk. Whether it's a shared idea or something experimental — I’m down to explore and create together. Let’s make something that feels real.
